Instructions, Question Formatting, and Registration Page
Instructions and Briefing
  1. The quiz assumes that you know bits of knowledge from every subject that you studied this year (Grade 9).
  2. There are 5 questions for 4 selected subjects in this school year, and 1 from 5 random subjects, totalling to a number of 30 questions for the entire quiz.
  3. They will consist of identification questions, checkbox questions, multiple choice questions, and more. There are no essays for easy computer checking. Text inputs must be typed in "Capitalize Each Word" form.
  4. Checkboxes have a really cool property -- you can select NONE of them because they are graded right minus wrong, and some questions are designed to have NO correct answer.
  5. Your answers save. Do not worry if you disconnect, because your your data will still be there once you reconnect!
  6. You can use the arrows in the left and right sides of the quiz in order to navigate through the quiz (you will see them later). Do not forget to submit each part of the quiz though, or you might get zeroes for that specific part of the quiz.
  7. Have fun, because you will have a very great time (if you did well or mediocre this school year)!
